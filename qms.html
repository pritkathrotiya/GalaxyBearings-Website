Quality Management System

To improve quality management system we have adopted IATF16949-2016 Quality system since February 2018. By the process of continual improvement we could achieve improvement in productivity, defect prevention, reduction of Variation & waste in supply chain & manufacturing processes. We have set up highly sophisticated plant having SPMs with a Capability to produce Bearings along with in-house Laboratory facility to fulfill the customer's requirements.


Galaxy Bearings Ltd. is committed in building a quality organization with Customer satisfaction as the core focus. We provide products and services which adequately meet customer requirements thereby ensuring their satisfaction.


Galaxy Bearing Ltd. achieved license of IS certification from Bureau of Indian Standard for Taper Roller Bearing T3DC055 Normal Class (32211) as per IS 12102:1987. We are first Bearing manufacturing company to get IS certification in India.
